Plugin for adding a highly customizable toolbar with color selection, social network icons, recent tweet and share buttons into footer.

== Description ==

WP Social Toolbar will display your latest tweet, list your social networking profiles, and allow users to "Like" your page on Facebook or tweet about it on Twitter! This plugin is also heavily changeable. You can specify colors and fonts, and rearrange your list of social network icons to your desire. Display it on the whole site or just the home page or just the blog or even just an indivdual page.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'wp-social-toolbar' folder to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
1. Put in all your custom settings in the WP Social Toolbar Settings page.
1. Insert all your social network links in the Social Profiles page.
